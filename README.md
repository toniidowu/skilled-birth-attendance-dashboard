# Skilled Birth Attendance in Katsina, Nigeria

By Toni Idowu | April 27, 2026

## Project Overview and Impact

This dashboard explores factors influencing skilled birth attendance among households surveyed in Katsina State, Nigeria as part of the CARE Nigeria EnRiCH program baseline survey.

Access to skilled birth attendance is critical for reducing maternal and newborn mortality, yet uptake varies widely across communities in northern Nigeria. This dashboard turns raw survey data into actionable insights, helping program managers and health officials identify gaps, target populations in need, and design more effective maternal health interventions in Katsina State.

## Contents of the Report

Pie Chart: Geographic distribution of study respondents across three local government areas (Charanchi, Katsina, and Mani).

Bar Chart: Skilled birth attendance rates by key characteristics (education level, occupation, local government area, perceived mortality, and community health worker engagement).
